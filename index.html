    Dive Into Our Courses & Witness The Wonder

    Beginner

    (5-15 years)

    Introduce your kids to the captivating world of chess! Start with the fundamentals such as

    chessboard, the distinct movements of each piece, and their values. Then delve into the core ideas of chess openings, vital attack & defense techniques and the principles of exchange. With a strong understanding of basic tactics and the necessary rules, they'll be well-equipped to start their chess journey and discover the game's incredible potential.

    Intermediate

    (5-15 years)

    Enhance your understanding of strategy & planning while diving into essential opening concepts.

    Improve your calculation and visualization skills, analyze various positions, make sound decisions and gain valuable insights for tournament play.

    Advanced

    (5-15 years)

    Achieving mastery in chess involves a deep understanding of numerous aspects including

    tactical patterns, positional play, and the intricacies of both minor and major piece endgames, as well as pawn endgames and their combinations. It also requires honing skills in making complex decisions and managing time wisely.

    Individual / 1-1 Classes

    Discover personalized chess improvement with our individual lessons

    that adapt to your timing needs. Enjoy the benefits of one-on-one tutoring, where we can target your key areas and weaknesses effectively.
