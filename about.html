About us

Our Coach

Ajay Bharadwaj, an enthusiastic chess player and dedicated chess instructor, has been imparting chess knowledge since 2017. He has a deep understanding of chess concepts, strategies and positions. His enthusiasm for teaching has helped young chess lovers, moulding them into an Astute players.
He consistently maintains an online rating over 2100 and is a certified ACM player with a FIDE rating. His primary goal is to promote and educate children in chess, helping them realize their ambition of becoming chess masters.

Why Astute Chess Academy?

Our expert tutor will guide you in unleashing your full potential and achieving your goals. By offering online instruction, we've been able to reach young minds around the world. We provide both one-on-one sessions and small group classes to ensure each student receives personalized attention.
Our live, interactive lessons are supplemented with audio, video, and recorded content. We also equip students with specially curated study materials and books. To further develop their skills, we assign regular game analyses and complex puzzles for homework.
This comprehensive approach empowers students to make the most of our online teaching platform and realize their academic aspirations.
